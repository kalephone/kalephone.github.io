Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is KalePhone?

KalePhone is a mobile application that helps you track and manage your phone usage habits to promote digital wellbeing and mindful technology use.

How does KalePhone work?

KalePhone monitors your phone usage patterns and provides insights and gentle reminders to help you maintain a healthy balance between screen time and other activities using Apple's standard Screen Time feature.

Why do I have to provide access to Screen Time?

This allows the app to see your activity data, restrict contents, and limit the usage of apps and websites.

Is my data private?

Yes, all your usage data is stored locally on your device. We don't collect or share any personal information with third parties.

Can I customize notification settings?

Yes, you can fully customize when and how you receive reminders and notifications through the notification settings.

Does KalePhone work in the background?

Yes, KalePhone runs in the background to track usage, but is designed to use minimal battery and system resources.
